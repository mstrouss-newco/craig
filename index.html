Everything in one place

The work between board meetings, handled

Stop chasing votes over email and digging through inboxes for the latest deck. craig gives your board a single source of truth.

📅

Meetings

Schedule the next meeting, track RSVPs, and watch a live quorum count so you always know whether you can do business.

💰

Grants

Move a grant to a vote, collect each director's decision, and see who has responded. It approves automatically the moment votes reach quorum.

✍

Signatures

Gather sign-offs on resolutions and consents with a clean record of who signed and when, including unanimous written consents.

📄

Documents

Share decks, minutes, and policies with a link, tied to the meeting they belong to so nothing gets lost.

💬

Updates

Post short notes to the board between meetings, so no one is caught off guard when everyone gets back in the room.

🏛

Governance

Keep the roster, roles, terms, and status tidy and current, the foundation everything else relies on.
